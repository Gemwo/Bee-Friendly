Bees in
Summer

This is the main pollen collecting season. The hive will be at maximum capacity. Flying worker bees go out and fetch the pollen, storing it in sacs on their legs to take to the hive. They give it to worker bees in the hive who then store it in cells in the hive. Saving it up for winter.
