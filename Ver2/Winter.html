Bees in
Winter


In winter there are about 10,000 bees remaining in the hive, these bees surround the queen to keep her warm. They do not leave the hive until temperatures get above 10 degrees celcius.

Interestingly, the lifespan of a winter bee is approximately 4-6 months. 3-5 months longer than the summer average. Prior to winter, bees are fed more royal jelly to increase their lifespan during this time!
